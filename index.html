My Journey

Born in 1984 in Kranj, Slovenia. Grew up in an entrepreneurial family, played professional basketball, studied in the USA (Fordham University + Harvard Business School), and built international startups.

I became Slovenia’s most successful Kickstarter creator with projects like FlyKly Smart Wheel (raised over $700k) and Smart Ped. Today I run BizMe, helping founders raise millions through crowdfunding, and lead Kenmare DAO in Web3 investments.
